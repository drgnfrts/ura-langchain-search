## Tender closing for URA sale site at Orchard Boulevard
---
title: Tender closing for URA sale site at Orchard Boulevard

link: https://www.ura.gov.sg/Corporate/Media-Room/Media-Releases/pr24-04

date: 01 February 2024

---

-----------------------------------------------------

  Published: 01 February 2024

The Urban Redevelopment Authority (URA) closed the tender for the site at Orchard Boulevard today.   
    
The site at Orchard Boulevard was launched for public tender on 17 October 2023.   
  
Please see [Annex A](https://www.ura.gov.sg/-/media/Corporate/Media-Room/2024/Feb/pr24-04a.pdf)  for details of the site and tender bids received.   
    
This is not an announcement of tender award. A decision on the award of the tender will be made after the bids have been evaluated. This will be publicised at a later date.
